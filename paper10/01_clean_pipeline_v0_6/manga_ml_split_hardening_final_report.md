# MaNGA M/L split hardening test — final report

Quality cut: `Qual >= 1`. Sample: 5,952 galaxies. Eight equal-count stellar-mass bins, 744 galaxies per bin, 186 galaxies in each young/old quartile. Primary age clock: `T50`; larger means older assembly.

## Plain-English result

The raw DML signal is mostly carried by the SPS stellar M/L denominator. Raw JAM dynamical M/L goes the opposite way: old galaxies have higher dynamical M/L than young galaxies in all eight mass bins. However, after controlling for SPS M/L, metallicity, mass, size, surface density, Sersic shape, flattening, lambda_R, and sigma, a smaller dynamical-age residual appears in the EP-favorable direction.
